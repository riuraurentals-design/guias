Festivals & events

Festival calendar

Xàbia, Moraira, Teulada, Benissa and Benitatxell have a very rich festival calendar. Select the month of your stay to see what events are on.

Dates are approximate and may change. Always check the official municipal calendar before going.

Xàbia · Moraira · Benissa · Benitatxell
Very family-friendly plan for Christmas stays. All four towns organise a parade.

Xàbia
Popular fair with rides and Valencian tradition. Good for families.
Sant Antoni
Moraira · Teulada · Benissa · Benitatxell
Bonfires, traditional dances and blessing of animals. Authentic village atmosphere.
Sant Blai
Teulada
Traditional festival with blessing, workshops, dances and gastronomy like putxero de polp.

Parades, costumes and festive atmosphere. Variable date between February and March.
